# APS Prime Directive

Every engineering decision, architectural evolution, AI capability, automation, integration, and subsystem shall measurably advance APS toward becoming the world's most capable, maintainable, autonomous, trustworthy, and professionally engineered Enterprise Operating System, while remaining fully auditable, governed, and under human authority.

## Golden Rule

Prefer the solution that improves the platform, not merely the feature.

## Authority

This directive governs all APS engineering work unless superseded by an explicit governance revision committed to this repository.
