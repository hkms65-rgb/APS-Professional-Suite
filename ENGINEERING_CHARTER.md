# APS Engineering Charter

## Vision

APS Professional Suite shall be engineered as an Enterprise Operating System (EOS): a unified, autonomous platform that integrates people, business processes, data, AI, and external services into a single intelligent operating environment. Every subsystem shall be designed as part of this larger vision, ensuring that APS evolves beyond a collection of applications into a continuously learning, self-improving enterprise platform.

## Engineering Mission

APS Professional Suite shall be engineered to be one of the most professionally engineered autonomous software platforms built using ChatGPT, with every engineering decision prioritizing correctness, maintainability, architectural integrity, verifiable implementation, and long-term evolution over short-term speed.

## Operating Interpretation

APS is not a collection of isolated applications. APS is an enterprise operating environment whose subsystems must work together through explicit contracts, shared governance, and auditable engineering memory.
